What is Discord used for, exactly?

All about: Discord calls, profiles, servers, and more!

Discord Calls/Video Chats

Discord is a service used for communicating with others. Their calling service truly does outrank most other services.
They host a user-friendly interface, having different buttons that would be easily understandable to a new user.

Call Buttons

The call buttons listed (left to right) are:

  • VIDEO TOGGLE

    • Gives the user the choice of turning their camera on or off.

  • AUDIO OUTPUT

    • Offers 'Speaker' and 'Phone' options. 'Speaker' is louder, and 'Phone' plays like a regular call.

  • MICROPHONE TOGGLE

    • Toggles your microphone on and off.

  • END CALL

    • With the simple press of a button, your call will come to a stop.

Your Profile

You are a unique individual. Make your profile as unique as you are! Discord lets users change...

  • STATUSES

    • Timed snippets of what you're thinking. Can add emojis to truly express yourself. Or, if you think that way all the time,
    there is a 'Don't Clear' option.

  • ABOUT ME

    • Your about me is your bio. Anything like pronouns, names, websites, or even silly things can be added on there!

  • PROFILE PICTURES

    • This describes who you are, essentially. Who people will see you as on the internet. With free Discord,
    users can choose still images (jpg, jpeg, png, etc) as their photo. Discord Nitro users, however, can use both still images andgifs

  • PROFILE BANNER

    • Profile banners are yet another way to express yourself. With free Discord, users can only choose a color for their profile banner.
    However, with Discord Nitro, users can both use still images (jpg, jpeg, png, etc.) and gifs.

Servers

Servers are a great way to meet other people who share your same interests.

To join a server...

  1. Get the join link of the server you want to join.
  2. Confirming that it isn't expired, it should bring you to a page that has "[user] has invited you to join [server name]".
  3. Click "Accept Invite" to be officially brought into the server.

If the link is expired, ask admin for new link.

To create a server...

  1. Click the plus sign to the right of your screen.
  2. You can choose from a template given, or create one from scratch.
  3. After that.. the world is your oyster! Add text/voice channels, roles, and more!

Groupchats

Groupchats are essentially smaller servers. They can hold up to 10 people and are great for just about everything.

How to make a groupchat...

  1. Go to the "Direct Messages" section of the application.
  2. Click this button.
  3. Add 2 or more people to get the party started!
