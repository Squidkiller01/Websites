What are Food Pantries and their goals


Food pantries are locations set up to feed those who may not have food or the right amount to feed themselves/their families. Food pantries are especially needed, where food is donated and given out to said people at no cost. With covid-19, some have lost their jobs and cannot provide as much for their families as before. Food pantries often accept volunteers to help give out food and pack food. However, it's different for everyone. Check with your local food pantry to see about volunteering with them.
