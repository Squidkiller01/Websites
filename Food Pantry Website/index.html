Volunteer Information

How To Volunteer

When volunteering you will be doing one of two things; you either serve the people in need or pack the boxes that are given to them. To serve the people you will be in a conveyor belt line, and passing the food up the chain and to the workers who will then pack the food into the peoples cars. When packing boxes, you will either be packing small or large boxes, there will be a specified list of items inside each box.
